Resort island with beaches, attractions, and Universal Studios Singapore

Victorian-era food centre in the heart of downtown. The Satay Street opens in the evenings

Frequently Asked Questions

When is the deadline to RSVP?

Please RSVP by 31 October 2025.

What should I wear?

We would recommend formal wear.

Will there be vegetarian options?

Yes, Claudine offers excellent vegetarian dishes. Please mention any dietary restrictions when you RSVP.

Can I bring a plus one?

Plus ones are by invitation only due to venue capacity. Please contact us if you're unsure.

What time should I arrive?

Please arrive by 18:30 in order to allow sufficient time for seating and greeting.

How should we get to Claudine?

The restaurant is most accessible by car but the closest bus stop is just a short five-minute walk away. We will likely be arranging a transfer service to and from the JEN Singapore Tanglin (Orchard Boulevard MRT) so please let us know if you would be interested.

How would you recommend we get around Singapore?

Public transport is highly recommended. Buses and trains are efficient and accept contactless cards. Taxis and ridesharing services are offered by apps such as Grab, CDG Zig, Gojek, and TADA.

Where would you recommend we stay?

The wedding venue is close to Orchard Road but Singapore is small enough that staying anywhere on the island is fine. Please reach out if you need help booking accommodation.

Can I join some of the weekend events even if I live in Singapore?

Yes, we welcome all friends and family to join the celebrations! Come make some new friends.
