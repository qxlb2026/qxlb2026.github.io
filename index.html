Travel & Accommodation

Recommended Hotel

JEN Singapore Tanglin by Shangri-La

A modern hotel located in the heart of Singapore's Orchard Road shopping district, close to the wedding venue.

Getting to Singapore

Fly into Singapore Changi Airport (SIN), one of the world's best airports with excellent facilities and connections.

Getting Around

Car sharing apps: Grab, CDG Zig, TADA, or Gojek provide convenient transportation. Singapore's excellent public transportation system (MRT and buses) accepts contactless credit cards directly.

Things to Do in Singapore

Must-See Attractions

  • Gardens by the Bay - Iconic supertree structures and conservatories
  • Marina Bay Sands SkyPark - Stunning city views from the 57th floor
  • Singapore Botanic Gardens - UNESCO World Heritage site with beautiful orchids
  • Chinatown & Little India - Cultural districts with authentic food and shopping

Shopping

  • Orchard Road - Premier shopping street with luxury boutiques
  • VivoCity - Large shopping mall near Sentosa Island
  • Haji Lane - Trendy street with unique local boutiques

Dining Recommendations

  • Newton Food Centre - Famous hawker center with local favorites
  • Lau Pa Sat - Historic Victorian market with diverse food options
  • Burnt Ends - Award-winning barbecue restaurant in Chinatown
  • Odette - Fine dining at the National Gallery Singapore
  • Try local dishes: Hainanese Chicken Rice, Laksa, Char Kway Teow

Frequently Asked Questions

What should I wear?

Cocktail attire is recommended. Think elegant evening wear - suits for men and cocktail dresses for women.

Will there be vegetarian options?

Yes, Claudine offers excellent vegetarian dishes. Please mention any dietary restrictions when you RSVP.

Can I bring a plus one?

Plus ones are by invitation only due to venue capacity. Please check your invitation or contact us if you're unsure.

What time should I arrive?

Please arrive by 5:45 PM for the 6:00 PM ceremony. This allows time for seating and greeting.

Is there parking available?

Valet parking is available at the restaurant. Street parking is also available in the surrounding area.

Will there be photography?

We will have a professional photographer capturing the special moments. We'll share photos with guests after the wedding.
